Frequently Asked Questions

Our progression system is designed to help you improve gradually:
  1. Start with an initial benchmark test to assess your current strength level
  2. The app creates a personalized training plan based on your results
  3. Each workout gradually increases in difficulty
  4. You can adjust the progression rate in settings

Push-ups are one of the most effective bodyweight exercises with numerous benefits:
  • Full-body workout: Engages multiple muscle groups including chest, shoulders, triceps, and core
  • Improved strength: Builds upper body and core strength efficiently
  • Better posture: Strengthens the muscles that support good posture
  • Cardiovascular health: Helps improve heart health when done with intensity
  • Increased bone density: Provides resistance that strengthens bones
  • Joint health: Stabilizes the shoulder joints and enhances mobility
  • Convenience: Requires no equipment and can be done anywhere
  • Adaptability: Can be modified to suit any fitness level
  • Functional fitness: Improves performance in daily activities

Regular push-up training with Pushup Viking helps you achieve these benefits through structured progression and consistent practice.

You can customize your workouts in several ways:
  • Adjust the number of sets (1-10)
  • Modify rest periods between sets
  • Choose between automatic or manual progression
  • Set up custom reminders and notifications

To restore your purchase:
  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Restore Purchase"
  4. Enter your Apple ID password if prompted

Pushup Viking offers three types of awards:
  • Strength Awards: Earned by reaching specific push-up milestones (100, 500, 1000, etc.)
  • Consistency Awards: Earned by maintaining workout streaks (1 week, 2 weeks, 10 weeks, 1 year)
  • Special Awards: Earned for unique achievements like early morning workouts, night workouts, or completing special challenges

The app provides several ways to track your progress:
  • Visual progress graphs showing your push-up counts over time
  • Weekly and monthly statistics
  • Achievement tracking with detailed progress towards each award
  • Personal best records for different workout types
  • Export your data for external analysis (Premium feature)

Missing a workout won't set you back significantly. The app is designed to be flexible:
  • Your streak will reset, but you can start a new one
  • Your strength progress is preserved
  • You can adjust your workout schedule to better fit your routine
  • The app will help you get back on track with appropriate workout recommendations

  • All core features are available without internet
  • Your workouts and progress are saved locally
  • Data syncs automatically when you're back online
  • You can still earn awards and track progress offline
